# 📢 Marketing Campaign Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Marketing Campaign Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze customer demographics, campaign performance, income distribution, spending behavior, and engagement metrics.

The dashboard provides valuable insights into customer profiles, marketing effectiveness, purchasing behavior, and campaign outcomes, helping businesses optimize marketing strategies and improve customer targeting.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate marketing campaign performance.
- Monitor customer engagement and web activity.
- Identify high-value customer segments.
- Analyze spending patterns across education levels and countries.
- Support data-driven marketing decisions.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Customer Demographics
- The average customer age is 54.2 years.
- Married customers generate a larger share of total income compared to single customers.
- Education levels significantly influence customer income and spending behavior.

### Geographic Analysis
- Spain generated the highest total income among all countries.
- Saudi Arabia and Canada ranked among the top-performing markets.
- Income distribution varies considerably across countries.

### Spending Behavior
- Customers with Graduation degrees contributed the highest spending.
- Higher education levels generally showed stronger purchasing power.
- Spending patterns differ across customer segments.

### Campaign Performance
- Marketing campaigns achieved 665 accepted responses.
- Customer complaints remained relatively low compared to the total customer base.
- Campaign effectiveness can be evaluated through customer engagement metrics.

### Trend Analysis
- Income remained relatively stable throughout the year.
- Strong performance was observed during several peak months.
- Seasonal fluctuations were visible across monthly income trends.
